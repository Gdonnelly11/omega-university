About Us

  • Founded in 1890

    • Fun Fact: That is 5% of the town's population.
  • Stonewall Jackson might have been born here.
  • All of our professors speak ancient Greek or Latin.

Who We Are?

Omega University is one of the oldest, most influential Liberal Arts Universities in the Eastern United States. We boast Appalachia's preeminent Classics department specializing in Greek literature.

We pride ourselves on this storied history and hope to see you here soon!

Our Campus

Our main campus is situated right on the main street of scenic Weston, West Virgina. We just recently purchased the abandoned schoolhouse outside town and hope to rebuild that as a part of our five year campus improvement plan.
